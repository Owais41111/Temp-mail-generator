What is Disposable Email?

A disposable email address is a temporary email service that allows you to receive emails without using your real email address. These temporary addresses automatically expire after a set period, protecting your privacy and keeping your main inbox clean.

Perfect for one-time signups, testing services, or any situation where you need an email address but don't want to share your personal one.
